Our vision

At Toolsquare, we believe that a technical lab should have a very open and transparent culture, where people have direct access to the machines to work on projects they are excited about and where people help each other with advice.


We also believe that an open culture works best with clear rules. The Toolsquare platform helps workshops implement these rules by providing targeted access rights, a reservation tool, feedback possibilities, safety checklists and cost allocation.
